Our Mission

Our focus is generative AI—specifically, image generation. We read papers, run experiments, and work with diffusion models to understand how they work at a deeper level.

The long-term goal is to build our own model. Not just fine-tune existing ones, but create something from scratch that turns text into visuals.

Current Research

Data science and AI aren't separate disciplines—they feed into each other. Understanding how data moves and behaves is fundamental to building systems that actually work. That's the approach we take.

  • LLM Agents

These three areas aren't random picks. They connect. What we learn from one shapes how we approach the others.
