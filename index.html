Our Mission

We are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible with generative AI. Our interdisciplinary team combines expertise in machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and human-computer interaction to create intelligent systems that augment human creativity and productivity.

We believe in the power of open research and strive to contribute meaningful advancements to the global AI community through publications, open-source tools, and educational initiatives.

Current Research

Our work spans three core areas of generative AI:

  • Data Analytics: Developing novel methods for extracting insights from large-scale datasets to inform model training and evaluation.
  • Diffusion Models: Advancing image and video generation through improved architectures, training techniques, and controllability mechanisms.
  • LLM Agents: Creating intelligent agents powered by large language models that can reason, plan, and interact with complex environments.
