Our Mission

We are a research-driven organization committed to advancing the field of Machine Learning. Our mission is to discover new insights and develop software that makes a positive impact on the world. We have a particular passion for generative AI, especially image generation models. We continuously study cutting-edge techniques, explore academic papers, and experiment with diffusion models to push the boundaries of what's possible in AI-generated imagery.

Ultimately, our goal is to develop our own generative image model that can create stunning visuals from textual descriptions. We believe that by harnessing the power of AI, we can unlock new creative possibilities and inspire innovation across various industries.

Current Research

We believe that data science and AI are deeply interconnected disciplines. From both practical and academic perspectives, mastering data analytics is essential to understanding how AI systems learn, make decisions, and generate outputs. That's why we're focusing on research areas that form a cohesive foundation—each requiring deep learning and hands-on experience. We are currently concentrating on the following areas:

  • Data Analytics
  • Fine Tuning Diffusion Models
  • LLM-based AI Agents

These research topics are not isolated pursuits. By studying data analytics alongside diffusion models and LLM-based agents, we're building a comprehensive understanding that will enable us to create more sophisticated and impactful AI solutions. Each field informs the others, creating a synergy that drives innovation forward.
